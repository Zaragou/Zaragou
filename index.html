What is Antique ?

Antique is much more than just an esports team. Born from the desire to bring together exceptional players, Antique is the perfect synthesis between the heritage of the great names in video games and a resolutely modern vision of competition. The team stands out for its meticulous and thoughtful approach to the game. Each member of Antique is a legend in their field. Constantly evolving, the team capitalizes on its past experience to adapt and dominate the new challenges of esports. The DNA of Antique is based on the idea that the history of video games is an infinite source of inspiration and wisdom. This philosophy is reflected in the way they approach each match: rigorous preparation, flawless group cohesion and resilience in the face of challenges. The team seeks to combine wisdom and ambitions. Antique's objective is clear: to write the history of esports by dominating the biggest international competitions while embodying the values ​​of fair play, humility and respect for opponents. In every tournament, Antique fights to live up to its name, demonstrating that experience and wisdom are as powerful assets as agility and speed in the world of esports.
